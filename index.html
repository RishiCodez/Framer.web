FAQ

What is Notrai?

Notrai is a sleek, AI-powered note-taking app designed for efficiency and clarity. With smart organization, intuitive search, and seamless syncing, Notrai helps users capture, structure, and retrieve ideas effortlessly. Whether for study, work, or creativity, Notrai enhances productivity with a clean, minimalistic interface and intelligent features tailored for modern thinkers.

Is it easy to learn?

Yes! Notrai is incredibly intuitive, with a clean design and smart features that make capturing and organizing notes effortless.

Can I organize my notes easily?

Absolutely! Notrai offers smart organization features like tags, folders, and an intuitive search function, making it easy to find and manage notes.
